Eight Minutes of: Thunderbolts*

It has all the makings so far of Definitely a Marvel Movie:

  • ✅ Single-handedly kicking the ass of 8 guys with guns
  • ✅ Bad guys (or good guys idk it doesn't actually matter, does it?) who inexplicably run at the "hero" with batons instead of firing their guns
  • ✅ "Witty" banter from a completely unconcerned "hero"
  • ✅ Some mysterious science that could "destroy the world"
  • ✅ A scientist with a cryptic warning who dies before he can explain to the audience what's going on
  • ✅ At least one morally grey character who I'm probably supposed to remember from some other media
  • ✅ 3+ references to movies I haven't seen (I think?)
  • ✅ Captain Ameri-Guinea Pig
  • ✅ A government that has lost control of its CEOs
  • ✅ Julia Louis-Dreyfus
  • ✅ A super-hero dry spell that's going to be a problem when the aforementioned "world-destroying" science is inevitably let loose
