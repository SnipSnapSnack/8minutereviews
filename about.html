Who Runs This Joint?

Howdy! My name's Dave and I built this page in my parents' basement on a Pentium II with a 56k modem that my little sister keeps unplugging by picking up the phone. (RACHEL. I MEAN IT.)

I love movies, I love HTML, and I love the idea that you can read a movie review in less time than it takes to microwave a Hot Pocket. Hence the name: 8 Minute Reviews.

The Rules of This Site

  1. Reviews must be readable in 8 minutes or less.
  2. Every review gets a star rating, because numbers are confusing.
  3. No spoilers in the first paragraph. (After that, anything goes.)
  4. Be kind, rewind.
