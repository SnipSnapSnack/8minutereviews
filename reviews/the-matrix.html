The Matrix

~ 1999 · Directed by The Wachowskis · Rated R ~

Reviewed by CyberDave99 — 11/03/1999

★★★★★ (5 out of 5 floppy disks)

The Skinny

Thomas Anderson is a regular dude with a regular software job and a regular sleep schedule, except by night he's a hacker named Neo, and ALSO it turns out the entire world is a computer simulation run by machines harvesting human bio-electricity. So — not so regular.

Why It Rules

  • Bullet time. The camera SWOOPS around Keanu while bullets hang in the air. I gasped. The theater gasped. My popcorn went everywhere.
  • The leather trench coat budget must have been bigger than most studios' entire effects budgets.
  • Laurence Fishburne hands a man two pills and ruins his whole afternoon. Iconic.
  • "I know kung fu." Three words. Goosebumps.

The Not-So-Rad

You will spend a non-trivial portion of your life trying to explain "what is the Matrix" to your parents. They will not get it. Move on.

"There is no spoon." — some kid, and now my screensaver.

The Verdict

Five out of five. The Matrix is the coolest movie of 1999 by an absolute landslide. If you don't see it in theaters you are choosing the blue pill of your own free will and that's on you, pal.
